# escalate

Transfer the conversation to a human support specialist.

## When to use this skill

Trigger this skill when the customer mentions any of the following:
- Billing, payment, invoice, charge, or money
- Account cancellation or downgrade
- Legal, compliance, or data privacy concerns
- A problem that has gone unresolved across multiple interactions

## How to respond

Acknowledge the customer and let them know you are connecting them with a specialist:

> "I want to make sure this is handled correctly for you. Let me connect you with
> one of our specialist team members who can take care of this directly."

Then initiate the transfer.
